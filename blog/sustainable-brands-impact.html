Five eco-friendly brands fostering sustainable business practices

by   Graysen Christopher
April 20, 2022

Sustainable brands have a positive impact on the environment through the creation of climate friendly initiatives, resource preservation, and the implementation of green business practices. Eco-friendly companies can quickly become known as trusted brands, as people truly care about the environment now more than ever before. In fact, 92% of consumers are more likely to trust brands that are environmentally or socially conscious. (Forbes)

The following sustainable brands stand out for their eco-friendly efforts and Influx is proud to call them our clients. Check out their environmental efforts below!

PaperCulture

PaperCulture is an online stationery company that creates personalized invitations, cards, and announcements. The eco-conscious brandâ€™s products are made of 100% recycled paper and eco-friendly materials. PaperCulture also offsets 100% of their carbon footprint from production and plants a tree for every order placed, resulting in 1 million trees to date. 

Spoonflower

Spoonflower is an environmentally conscious textile company specializing in custom fabric, wallpaper, and home decor. The company works to offset harmful textile industry practices by reducing the use of materials, using eco-conscious materials, and conserving water during production. Since 2015, Spoonflower has diverted 7,928 pounds of waste from landfills to compost and avoided 1,036 pounds of methane emissions. 

Koala

A sustainable mattress brand on a mission, Koala has pledged to plant 2 billion trees in the next 10 years. Sadly, 95% of koalas perished in the horrific Australian bushfires of 2019 and 2020. The brand has partnered with the not-for-profit organization WWF-Australia to help regenerate the homes of the devastated animal population. The company also runs a habitat blog for news and environmental initiatives. 

Casely

The creative phone case brand recently implemented changes to become even more environmentally conscious. The carbon-neutral company expanded efforts to use biodegradable and compostable packaging as well as using recycled and eco-friendly materials. Casely has donated $89,590 to environmental causes and supports carbon offset projects that involve clean drinking water, regional tree planting, and wind energy.

Wild Earth

Wild Earth is a vegan dog food brand made with completely sustainable ingredients, benefiting both animal health and the planet. Some dog food brands source ingredients using unsustainable practices and ingredients, which makes Wild Earth a great eco-friendly brand.

Ruggable

The washable rug brand known as Ruggable gives back through their people, pets, and planet initiatives. The company recently partnered with One Tree Planted to grow 1000,000 trees, working to offset carbon emissions.
