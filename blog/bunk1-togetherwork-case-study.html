Bunk1 case study: 8 dedicated agents handle 5,000 tickets/month with 56% decrease in missed calls

About Bunk1 by Togetherwork:

Bunk1 is an online platform used by summer camps across the United States and Canada that offers families a simple way of staying connected with their campers. It’s also used as a camp management module for camp administrators. The brand was acquired by Togetherwork, a group management software and payment company that has experienced tremendous growth over the past few years.

With Bunk1, families can access secure online photo galleries with automated camper photo tagging, send printed notes to their camper, read daily newsletters, and watch videos from camp from a desktop browser and the easy-to-use mobile app for iOS and Android devices.

Influx team setup:

  • 8 dedicated agents
  • Voice support, email, and in-app coverage
  • US coverage: 12 hrs/7 days a week

Influx team results:

  • 56% decrease in missed calls
  • 11% CSAT increase
  • Inbox zero achieved weekly
  • Scaled from 1 agent to 8 agents

Getting started with Influx:

Bunk1 came to Influx ready to ramp up customer support for peak season from May-August when ticket spikes hit 5,000 tickets/month on average. The brand needed a support team to handle increased coverage and allow their internal team to focus on the business. 

Within a month, Bunk1 scaled from 1 dedicated agent to 8 dedicated agents to handle 50 tickets/day for voice support, email, and in-app coverage. The brand easily scaled up to 8 agents for peak season and down to 2 agents as the summer wrapped up in August. 

What they’re saying about Influx:

“Bunk1 has experienced serious growth and wanted a customer support partner that could handle summer ticket spikes when camps are in session. It was great to start off with one agent and increase that number as needed.

Influx was easy to work with and quickly trained more agents to meet our needs. Plus, we saw an increase in CSAT and decrease in missed calls!”

- Kathryn Hamilton, Senior Director of Operations at Togetherwork
